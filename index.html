    Glossary

    Health Score (0–100)

    Weighted composite of 5 signals: Support Tickets (20%) — fewer HIGH tickets = better; Slack Activity (20%) — recent channel activity signals engagement; Onboarding Progress (30%) — Rocketlane task completion %; Engagement Recency (15%) — days since last email, meeting, or message; Blockers (15%) — fewer blockers = healthier. Sources that are unavailable have their weight redistributed.

    Onboarding %

    Percentage of Rocketlane project tasks completed. Reflects implementation progress across all phases. "N/A" means no Rocketlane project is linked to this client (e.g., they are already fully onboarded or use a different tracking system).

    Status

    On Track — Health ≥70, no HIGH tickets, active engagement. At Risk — Health <50, or 2+ HIGH tickets, or silent 5+ days. Stalled — Health 50–69, or silent 3–5 days, or 3+ overdue tasks. Expanding — Active upsell deal + Health ≥85.
